Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how FlashNews 24/7 Update (“we”, “us”, and “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website and how we collect, use, and protect information through these technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the site owners.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of our website and cannot be switched off in our systems. They enable basic functions like page navigation and secure areas.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often. The data is aggregated and anonymous, used to improve user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies: These remember choices you make to provide enhanced, personalized features, like your preferred language or region.
  • Targeting or Advertising Cookies: These cookies track browsing habits to deliver relevant advertisements through third-party platforms. We do not store personal information with these cookies.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us:

  • Provide optimal functionality and improve website performance.
  • Understand user preferences and tailor the experience accordingly.
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.
  • Ensure security and detect potential malicious activities.
  • Deliver relevant advertising based on interests.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third parties including analytics and advertising partners. These third parties have their own privacy policies and control over cookies.

Common third-party services we use may include Google Analytics, advertising networks, and social media platforms.
